Between North and South China
October 30, 2020

南北之间
十月三十日，二零二零年

Due to the differences in the geographical location, climatic characteristics, history & culture, customs, and political & economic activities of North and South China, the natural and cultural landscapes in the north and the south of China are significantly different.

This project explores the cultural differences between North and South China from a contemporary standpoint, specifically in the categories of architecture and food. It seeks to not only educate people who are interested in Chinese culture but also to examine how the differences can potentially play a factor within people’s relationship and communication. It is an interactive way to dissect the complex Chinese culture.

由于中国北方和南方的地理位置，气候特征，历史文化，习俗以及政治和经济活动的差异，北方和南方的自然和文化景观也有很大差异。

该项目将会从当代的角度出发，探讨北方和南方之间的文化差异，特别是在建筑和美食方面。这不仅提供给对中国文化感兴趣的人，并会研究此差异如何可能在人们的关系和沟通中有着影响。它是解剖复杂的中国文化的一种互动方式。
